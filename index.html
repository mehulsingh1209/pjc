About Us

Your Trusted Recruitment Partner

Our Mission

Established in 2014, Parsa Job Centre has been at the forefront of employment services in Birgunj and throughout Nepal. Our mission is to bridge the gap between all types of candidates and quality employers, supporting economic growth and career development throughout the Nepal.

With deep roots in the local community and an extensive network spanning all seven provinces, we understand the unique challenges and opportunities in Nepal's labor market. Our dedicated team of recruitment specialists works tirelessly to match the right talent with the right opportunities, ensuring both employers and job seekers achieve their goals.

At Parsa Job Centre, we're not just filling positions; we're building careers and strengthening businesses through strategic talent acquisition and development.

Job Placement & Matching

We connect qualified candidates with suitable employers based on skills, experience, and company requirements. Our personalized matching process ensures the right fit for both parties, considering cultural fit, career goals, and growth potential for long-term success.

Skill Development & Training

We offer comprehensive skill development programs tailored to industry demands. Our training modules include technical skills, soft skills, and professional development workshops that help candidates enhance their employability and prepare for success in today's competitive job market.

Support for International Job Placements

We provide end-to-end assistance for Nepali workers seeking opportunities abroad. Our services include documentation support, pre-departure training, cultural orientation, and ongoing support throughout the international employment process, ensuring safe and beneficial overseas employment.
